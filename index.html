Inbhreathnaitheach

Beyond the Misty Mountains, through the Dark Forest at the heart of all of Faoi Woods lies a bear den below a willow tree. The dark burrow appears desolate and endless, but if you don't stray from the path and keep crawling: you will see a exit hole that floods the end of the den with blue light. Crawl through and feast your eyes upon the majesty of Inbreathnaitheach

A land of eternal night completely inhabited by Bears. Upon exiting the tunnel, you will be greated by the leader of the elder council. He will bring you on a series of tests I can not disclose. But if you are to pass said tests, you will be granted access to stay in the kingdom of Inbreathnaitheach whenever and for as long as you want.

Feast your eyes upon the majesty of Bear country. Welcome traveler, you have found your home.
